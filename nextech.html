About Project NexTech

Project NexTech is an initiative focused on bridging the gap between students and emerging technologies. As an Executive Committee Member, I help plan and execute events, workshops, and programs designed to introduce students to cutting-edge fields in technology.

My Role

As part of the executive committee, my responsibilities include:

  • Planning and organizing technology-focused workshops and events
  • Collaborating with team members to develop educational content
  • Helping students discover opportunities in computer science and technology
  • Contributing to strategic decisions about the direction of the organization

Impact

Through Project NexTech, I've had the opportunity to help other students explore areas of technology they might not have encountered otherwise. It's been rewarding to combine my technical interests with leadership and community building.

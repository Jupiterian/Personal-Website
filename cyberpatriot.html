About CyberPatriot

Through CyberAegis and the CyberPatriot competition, I have spent significant time hardening Windows systems and services. A major part of the competition is learning how to remediate existing damage while also preventing future issues from occurring.

My focus areas

Across multiple seasons, I have worked across Windows security, threat remediation, and prevention. That mindset has helped my teams perform consistently well in competition.

  • Windows system hardening and security configuration
  • Active Directory management and security
  • Service hardening and access control
  • PowerShell scripting for security automation
  • Threat remediation and vulnerability patching

Giving back

More recently, I have started creating content to help middle school students enter cybersecurity and understand the fundamentals of Windows hardening. Helping the next generation of competitors get started has been especially rewarding.
